Diana Life

  • Original name Diana Frances Spencer
  • Born July 1, 1961, Sandringham, Norfolk, England
  • She renewed her contacts with the royal family, and her friendship with Charles grew in 1980
  • On February 24, 1981, their engagement was announced, and her beauty and shy demeanour—which earned her the nickname “Shy Di”
  • The couple married in St. Paul’s Cathedral on July 29, 1981, in a globally televised ceremony watched by an audience numbering in the hundreds of millions.
  • Their first child, Prince William Arthur Philip Louis of Wales, was born on June 21, 1982
  • Their second, Prince Henry (“Harry”) Charles Albert David, on September 15, 1984
  • The couple formally separated in 1992.
  • After the divorce, Diana maintained her high public profile and continued many of the activities she had earlier undertaken on behalf of charities, supporting causes as diverse as the arts, children’s issues, and AIDS patients. She also was involved in efforts to ban land mines.
  • Diana was passed away, along with her companion, Dodi Fayed, and their driver, Henri Paul, in an automobile accident in a tunnel under the streets of Paris in 1997
